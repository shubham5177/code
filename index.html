Problems Faced by Farmers

1. Lack of Irrigation Facilities

Most farmers depend on rainfall, making their crops vulnerable to droughts.

2. Soil Degradation

Overuse of fertilizers and chemicals has reduced soil fertility.

3. Market Access Issues

Farmers often fail to get fair prices for their produce due to lack of market knowledge and middlemen.

Solutions Offered by Krushi Mitra

1. Promoting Rainwater Harvesting

Encourage farmers to build small ponds and use drip irrigation systems to optimize water usage.

2. Organic Farming Practices

Provide training on composting and the use of organic fertilizers to rejuvenate the soil.

3. Digital Market Platforms

Help farmers connect with buyers directly through mobile apps and e-marketplaces.
